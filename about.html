About Our San Diego Machine Shop

45+ Years of Precision Machining Excellence

Our San Diego Story

Founded in 1980, Qualified Machine Shop has been a cornerstone of precision machining and custom manufacturing in San Diego for over four decades. From our roots as a small, family-owned business, we have grown alongside San Diego's booming technology sectors to become a trusted partner for companies across aerospace, medical, automotive, and defense industries.

Our commitment to quality and customer service has made us one of the most reliable machine shops in Southern California. Located in the Sorrento Valley tech hub, we combine traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology to deliver exceptional results for local businesses and national clients.

Today, we continue to push the boundaries of manufacturing, holding true to our founding principles of quality, integrity, and innovation right here in San Diego.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every project we undertake

F

First Article Inspection

We perform thorough First Article Inspection (FAI) before running production to ensure every part meets specifications from the very first piece.

Q

Quality Collaboration

We work closely with customers to identify and resolve potential errors in prints, ensuring dimensions and specifications work in real-world applications.

O

On-Time Delivery

We are committed to meeting deadlines and delivering your parts on schedule, every time.

C

Clear Communication

We maintain excellent communication throughout every project, keeping you informed and ensuring your complete satisfaction.

Our Expert San Diego Team

Our skilled team of CNC machinists, programmers, and quality control specialists brings decades of combined experience to every project. We are proud to be a local San Diego employer committed to upholding the highest standards of precision and quality.
