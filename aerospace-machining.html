Industries We Serve in San Diego

Precision Manufacturing for Diverse Applications

Trusted Across Multiple Industries

For over 45 years, Qualified Machine has been the go-to precision machining partner for San Diego's most demanding industries. Our experience spans aerospace, medical devices, automotive, technology, and more.

We understand that each industry has unique requirements, and our skilled team brings decades of combined experience to every project. From prototype to production, we deliver precision parts that meet your exact specifications.

Aerospace & Defense

Critical flight hardware and defense components manufactured with precision and full traceability. We machine exotic alloys like Titanium and Inconel for San Diego's aerospace sector.

  • Structural airframe components
  • Engine parts and housings
  • Brackets and fixtures
  • Complex geometries

Medical Devices

FDA-compliant medical device components for San Diego's thriving biotech industry. We work with biocompatible materials and maintain rigorous quality control.

  • Surgical instruments
  • Implantable components
  • Diagnostic equipment parts
  • Biocompatible materials (Titanium, PEEK)

Automotive & Racing

  • Performance engine components
  • Custom drivetrain parts
  • Racing fixtures and tooling
  • EV components
